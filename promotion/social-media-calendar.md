## Content Guidelines

### Best Practices
1. **Be genuine** - Authenticity beats polish
2. **Provide value** - Don't just sell, help
3. **Engage** - Respond to all comments
4. **Be consistent** - Post regularly
5. **Track results** - See what works

### Avoid
1. ❌ Don't use clickbait
2. ❌ Don't over-sell
3. ❌ Don't ignore negative feedback
4. ❌ Don't copy competitor content
5. ❌ Don't use too many hashtags (max 3-5)

### Hashtag Strategy
For maximum reach, use a mix of:
- Product hashtags: #SaaS #B2B #EmailMarketing
- Audience hashtags: #Entrepreneur #Founder #MarketingManager
- Topic hashtags: #AI #Productivity #ColdEmail
- Trending hashtags: Check what's trending daily
